---
title: "Submit KYB"
openapi: "POST /v1/me/kyb"
---

Submit Know Your Business (KYB) verification documentation. Required before your tenant can process transfers.

## Business Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `fintech` | Licensed fintech / payment company |
| `bank` | Licensed bank or banking institution |
| `payment_processor` | Payment processor / PSP |
| `other` | Other financial services |

After submission, your KYB status changes to `IN_REVIEW`. A Settla operations team member will verify the information and either approve (`VERIFIED`) or reject (`REJECTED`) the application.
