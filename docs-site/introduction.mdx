# Welcome to Settla

Settla provides cross-border payment infrastructure powered by stablecoin rails. We handle the complexity of FX quotes, smart routing, blockchain settlement, and treasury management so you can focus on your product.

## What Settla Does

- **Cross-border transfers** — Send money between countries via stablecoin rails (USDT, USDC on Tron, Ethereum, Polygon)
- **Smart routing** — Automatically selects the best route based on cost (40%), speed (30%), liquidity (20%), and reliability (10%)
- **Crypto deposits** — Accept on-chain stablecoin payments from your users
- **Bank deposits** — Accept fiat deposits via virtual bank accounts
- **Payment links** — Create shareable links for crypto payments
- **Real-time treasury** — Track balances, positions, and liquidity across all corridors
- **Automatic settlement** — Daily net settlement reduces N transfers to 1 net position per currency pair

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get a Quote">
    Request a real-time FX quote for your corridor. Quotes include fees, route details, and a 5-minute expiry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Transfer">
    Submit a transfer with the quote ID and recipient details. Settla handles the stablecoin settlement end-to-end.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Progress">
    Monitor transfer status via polling or webhooks. Each transfer moves through a well-defined state machine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Settlement">
    Funds are settled to the recipient. Daily net settlement optimizes for minimum on-chain transactions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
