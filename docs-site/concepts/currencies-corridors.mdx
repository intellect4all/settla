## Smart Routing

When you create a quote or transfer, Settla's router evaluates all available routes and scores them:

| Factor | Weight | Higher Score Means |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Cost | 40% | Lower total fees |
| Speed | 30% | Faster settlement |
| Liquidity | 20% | More available balance |
| Reliability | 10% | Higher historical success rate |

The highest-scoring route is selected automatically. Use the [routing options endpoint](/api-reference/routes/get-routing-options) to see all available routes with their scores.
